Tools

There are a few tools that you can use to make sure you keep your operating system running its best:

  • Install a paid version of a reputable anti-virus software
  • Run Windows system tools: Disk cleanup and Defrag
  • Use Qualys Browser Check for deficiencies in updates (see link below)
  • Install a paid version of anti-malware software
  • Install All Important Updates: Windows, Adobe, Java, Anti-virus, Installed Programs
  • Clean browser cookies and cache
  • Run Windows system tools: Disk cleanup and Defrag
  • Watch out for check boxes when installing new programs and updates
  • Stay away from untrusted websites
  • Don't click on links or open attachments from suspicious email
  • Give computers plenty of airflow from all sides
  • Blow out the dust with canned air or an air compressor every couple of months

Advice

The joy of owning a computer to use for business or for pleasure does not come without its responsibilities. In terms of hardware, it is important to keep your system well-ventilated and free of dust. Heat is your computer's biggest external enemy. In terms of software, it is important to keep your operating system and installed programs running as efficiently as possible and without obstruction of invisible resource hogs, infections, malware, and adware. Listed to the right are some simple tasks that you can take to keep your computer running...on task.
